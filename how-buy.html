Questions

Buyer FAQ

Do I need a real estate agent to buy?

You don’t have to bring your own agent — our team can guide you through touring, offers, and closing. If you already work with an agent, you’re welcome to involve them too.

How do self-guided tours work?

Pick a day and time, verify your identity once, and we’ll send check-in details so you can let yourself in. Tours are available seven days a week so you can visit on your schedule.

What does getting pre-qualified involve?

It’s a quick estimate of your buying power based on your income, debts, and down payment. It helps you set a realistic budget and makes your offer stronger. Start your pre-qualification →

Can I estimate my monthly payment before touring?

Yes. Every listing includes an instant monthly payment estimate, and you can fine-tune it with our mortgage calculator.
