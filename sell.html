What’s behind your estimated cash offer
We rely on comparable sales, current market conditions, and your home’s details. The assessment then confirms condition by checking for things like:
  • Permitted additions or modifications
  • Typical neighborhood noise levels
  • No damage from floods or natural disaster
  • No mold, smoke, or fire damage
This is a preliminary estimate, not a binding offer. Your final cash offer is confirmed after your home assessment. A team member will reach out within one business day. You can cancel for free any time before closing.
