Questions

Frequently asked questions

How is my cash offer calculated?

We consider your home’s location, size, condition, and recent comparable sales nearby, then make a competitive cash offer. The figure shown in the form is only a preliminary estimate — your real offer comes after we review your home.

Which areas do you buy in?

We currently purchase homes in Orange County and Los Angeles County, California. Enter your address in the form to instantly check whether we serve your area.

Do I need to make repairs?

No. We buy homes completely as-is, so there’s no need to clean, repair, or renovate before selling.

Are there any fees or commissions?

There are no agent commissions and no listing fees. We’ll walk you through any standard closing costs transparently before you commit.

How fast can I close?

Once you accept, you can often close in as little as a week — or choose a later date that works better for you.

Is the online estimate a guaranteed offer?

No. The online range is an informational estimate based on the details you provide. A formal, no-obligation offer is provided after our team evaluates the property.
